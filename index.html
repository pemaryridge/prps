Who We Are

At Pemary Ridge Primary School, we are committed to providing a nurturing and stimulating learning
environment where every child can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our Mission

Strive for excellence in the holistic development of all learners through quality teaching & learning in a safe environment. Aim to promote creativity & inculculate sound human values. Uphold religious tolerance & respect cultural diversity.

Our Vision

To empower every learner with sound human values and educational excellence to meet the challenges in a demanding global an transformational society.

Academic Excellence

Our dedicated team of educators is committed to delivering a comprehensive curriculum that challenges and engages students at every level. We embrace innovative teaching methods and technology to prepare our students for success.
